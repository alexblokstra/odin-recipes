Easy Homemade Pasta

This homemade pasta recipe is consistently great and easy to make with flour, eggs, salt, olive oil, and water. Roll out by hand or use a pasta machine to cut dough into desired pasta shape.

Ingredients

  1. Flour: This homemade pasta recipe starts with two cups of all-purpose flour.
  2. Eggs: Eggs lend fat and moisture to the pasta dough.
  3. Oil: Two tablespoons of olive oil add moisture and help the dough come together.
  4. Salt: A teaspoon of salt takes the flavor up a notch.
  5. Water: Add just enough water to form a smooth, thick dough.

Steps

  • Make the dough.
  • Turn the dough out and knead it for 10 minutes.
  • Let the dough rest.
  • Divide the dough into balls.
  • Use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough into desired shapes.
